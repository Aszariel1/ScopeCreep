A live demo project — click around, request a change, approve one. Nothing here is real client work.

Log every request

Organize change requests by category, per project, so nothing a client asks for slips through unbilled. Each category keeps its own running log, so you can see exactly what changed and when, without digging through old messages or emails to piece it back together.

Keep scope honest

Attach a price and an hours estimate to every approved change before it becomes real work. Totals and the estimated delivery date recalculate automatically, so you always know where a project actually stands.

Share with clients

Send one link, no account required on their end. Clients see live status, request changes themselves, and approve the ones with a price attached — all without a single back-and-forth email.
